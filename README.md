# Nemories

Nemories is a cozy desktop journaling application where every memory you write becomes part of a living top down world. As your journal grows, so does your little village (town/city if you like journalling a lot like me lol xD), creating a unique visual representation of your memories.
This was made as a submission to a Hackclub YSWS called ThingOnDesk. This can help anyone organise their thoughts and it can also help you realise that we aren't always going through hard times by seeing many memories of happiness and love :3

## Features

* Write and organize your daily memories.
* Every memory generates a unique building.
* Procedurally generated roads connect new memories.
* Infinite world that expands as you journal.
* Animated bird flocks that react to your cursor.
* Fireflies that wander around.
* Builtin journal with emotion selection.
* Automatic local saving, your memories stay on your computer and isn't shared with anyone but you and only you.
* Pie chart statistics for your memories that can also be changed into a widget.
* Graph that plots on the basis of memories added every day to show the ups and downs in your life.
* Satisfying hover sfx.
* Custom scroll bar.
* New generation every time you reload/restart app.
* Direct edit option if you click on a building.
